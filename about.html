Our Story

Building Bridges Through Faith & Community

Founded in 2003, the Muslim Community was born from a simple vision: to create a welcoming space where every Muslim — new or lifelong — could find brotherhood, sisterhood, knowledge, and support. What started as a small prayer circle of 12 families has grown into a thriving community of over 5,000 members across the region.

We believe that faith is not practiced in isolation — it flourishes when shared. Our community center is a place of worship, learning, celebration, service, and connection. From weekly Jumu'ah prayers to youth programmes, food banks to educational scholarships, we strive to serve every dimension of community life.

Rooted in the Quran and Sunnah, guided by compassion, and driven by a commitment to excellence, we are united in our pursuit of a community that reflects the true spirit of Islam.
